Key Legend

TAB - toggles legend on/off
Enter or Left mouse button - capture single photo
ESC or Center mouse button - toggle review last photo
Right mouse button - capture 4 photo collage
S - Single photo mode
C - Collage photo mode
Q - Exit Photobooth
Space Bar - immediate capture no countdown
Image Filters:
0 - No Filter
1 - Grayscale
2 - Level threshold 0.5
3 - Posterize 13
4 - Posterize 8
5 - Posterize 5
DEBUG:
M - Mirror toggle
P - Portrait mode
L - Landscape mode
D - Debug information to the console
